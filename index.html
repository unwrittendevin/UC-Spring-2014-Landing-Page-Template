There are many reasons Syracuse University is the best choice in Central New York for part-time study. Here are just a few:

  • Choice: More than 200 degree programs allow you to choose the degree you need for the career you want.
  • Coaching: Advisors are trained coaches who guide you through your academic regimen and help you overcome roadblocks to success.
  • Convenience: You can choose classes, register, and manage financial aid – all in one location
  • Affordability: Scholarships, grants, opportunity programs and financial guidance put a degree within your reach.
  • Reputation: You’ll become part of a top-ranked university with a global alumni network.
